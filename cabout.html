MAYAMBELAY SWIMMING POOL

Mayambelay or Maidmu Lake is a breathtaking man-made lake located in a picturesque setting. It was constructed from 2002-2004 EC by the Chinese enterprise called Satchone in cooperation with the Tigrian Development Association. Originally intended for irrigation purposes, the lake's location and its natural beauty transformed it into a spectacular attraction.

The Mayambelay Swimming Pool offers a serene and refreshing environment for visitors to enjoy. With crystal-clear waters and surrounded by lush vegetation, it's a paradise for nature lovers and those seeking relaxation. The pool is carefully designed to blend harmoniously with the surrounding landscape, providing a tranquil oasis for everyone to escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life.

The pool area is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including spacious changing rooms, comfortable lounging areas, and a dedicated children's section. Whether you're looking to swim, sunbathe, or simply unwind by the water, Mayambelay Swimming Pool has something for everyone.

In addition to its natural beauty, Mayambelay Swimming Pool offers a range of recreational activities. There are multiple water slides for thrill-seekers, providing an exhilarating experience for both children and adults. The pool also features a diving area with different diving platforms, catering to diving enthusiasts of all skill levels.

For those who prefer a more relaxing experience, the pool offers a luxurious spa section. Here, visitors can enjoy rejuvenating massages, soothing jacuzzis, and other spa treatments that will leave them feeling refreshed and pampered.

Mayambelay Swimming Pool takes great pride in its commitment to safety. Lifeguards are stationed around the pool at all times, ensuring the well-being of visitors. The pool follows strict hygiene and cleanliness protocols to provide a safe and hygienic environment for everyone to enjoy.

Whether you're a local resident or a tourist, a visit to Mayambelay Swimming Pool is an experience you won't want to miss. Immerse yourself in the natural beauty, enjoy the range of recreational activities, and unwind in the tranquil surroundings. Mayambelay Swimming Pool truly offers the ultimate aquatic getaway.
